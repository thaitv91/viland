Phoenix Cruise 2 Days 1 Night

Expectations of even the most demanding customers. The on board restaurant at the had of the junk will serve customers beautiful feasts and tastety fresh seafood. Come and enjoy!

Like many cruises of its class, Halong Phoenix Cruise is covered with a traditional Vietnamese style from outer to inner space. That style is formed by wooden furniture and decoration anywhere on the cruise, which are precisely settled to create not only unique but also romantic atmosphere for a night onboard.

This is not a large cruise with only 16 deluxe cabins including 8 double suites and 8 twin suites equipped with standardized amenities like 3-star hotel rooms on land. The featured element of accommodation on this cruise is each cabin contains ancient objects and decoration, like a “Dong Ho” painting or old-style shelves.

That enhances the “Vietnamese” characteristic on the board, and offers unique feeling to passengers when they are on the cruise. Beside these deluxe cabins, Halong Phoenix Cruise also offer numerous services with outstanding quality to passengers such as cozy dining rooms with panoramic windows and delicious menus, or airy sundeck to admire holistic landscape of the bay.

Day 1: Hanoi – Halong Bay (Lunch, Dinner, Guide)

After breakfast at your hotel, your guide will pick you up to cross the Red River delta, the second biggest in Vietnam and through the scenic countryside to reach Halong Bay.

You will arrive at Tuan Chau International Marina on the harbor of Halong’s biggest island. From here a tender boat will take you to our Halong Phoenix Luxury Cruiser. A refreshing welcome drink is served and you can check into your cabin.

In the afternoon, your boat sets sail to the centre of this World Heritage site.  Lunch is served in the restaurant while you enjoy the amazing scenery and delicious lunch of fresh local seafood or your requested vegetarian meal. Cruise amongst the hundreds of islands and gorgeous natural settings, limestone bedrocks and sheer cliffs of tiny islets. You will pass by Duck and Thumb islets, and then Coconut Island – the only soil island in the World Heritage Site.

At Luom Bo area (the provincial border between Quang Ninh province and Cat Ba National Park of Hai Phong), the boat stops and you will kayak in the turquoise waters here. Swimming at the secluded sandy beach is an option for people who are not interested in kayaking.

As the sun sets over the bay, you will head back to our cruise and relax. Coffee, tea and fruits will be available on the sundeck or you can order your favorite drink from the bar. The boat will continue to cruise to the Fairy Lake (Ho Dong Tien) area and anchor for the night.

Dinner is served with local seafood and then you may choose to enjoy a drink from the bar or experience squid fishing, before retiring.

Day 2: Halong Bay – Hanoi (Breakfast, Lunch, Guide)

Your breakfast is served from 7.30am until 8.30am. After that, you will visit an impressive grotto of Halong Bay – Surprising (Sung Sot) Cave, with your guide. A truly awesome and amazing cave to experience.

You will return to the Halong Phoenix Luxury cruiser to begin your return journey to the harbor and experience viewing other areas and islands of Halong Bay such as Fighting Cocks (symbol of Halong Bay), Incense Burner and Stone Dogs islands.

You will then enjoy a cooking demonstration onboard shows you how to make authentic Vietnamese spring rolls. You will learn about the recipes and the techniques that create this popular dish of the Vietnamese people.

After that, you’ll return to the junk for lunch while cruising back to the main dock and around noon you’ll leave for Hanoi. You will arrive at your hotel in Hanoi in late afternoon, and the remainder of the day will be your own time.

How do I book and pay for a tour ?

Once you have found a tour that you would like to book, please fill in your booking details (the departure date that you would like to go, the package, the number of people, your full name, nationality, email address and any special requests). Once we confirm the availability, we will send you an email to confirm and our payment policy information. With this, you can choose your preferred payment method and pay us.

With tour bookings, you DO NOT need to pay total in advance, we just need 30% for Deposit, to ensure that you use our services. With flight or train ticket bookings, we require 100% total payment in advance. If you would like to pay by others methods, please inquire with us directly.

What if I need to cancel or change my itinerary ?

We allow you to change your dates, if needed, at no additional cost as long as you give sufficient advance notice and availability exists.
Cancellations of the tours usually involve a cancellation fee, which depends on how far in advance that you cancel. For each tour, the cancellation and change policies depend on the status of services providing. Please inquire directly for the change or cancellation policy for any particular tour.

Do you offer longer or more customized tours ?

You can ask for longer tours or customize your favourite travels with any our tours. Tours on our site www.travelagencyvietnam.com are popular tours which can be changed or customized for your needs.

If you have questions about a particular tour or would like a longer or more customized tour, please contact us at booking@travelagencyvietnam.com, and our travel experts will help you find your best travel itinerary.

What if I have special needs for the tour ?

If you have special requirements – for example, if you have a large group or young children, or you have infants or the olds in your group, or if you are handicapped or have physical limitations, if you have special transportation needs, or if you are vegetarian or have other food allergies – please let us know, and we can work with both you and the service provider ( restaurant, hotels, transportation …) to ensure that we can meet your needs.

How can I book a hotel / resort room ?

Simply select the hotel in which you would like to stay (or email or call us with your requests and we will make recommendations). Once you have selected a hotel, proceed with “Select” and please fill out all customer and payment information, as well as any special requests. We will confirm the availability of the hotel, and send you payment details as with tour booking.

When do I need to pay for hotel / resort booking ?

All hotel/ resort reservations must be paid in advance and immediately after booking the hotel/resort. We cannot hold hotel/resort bookings without payment. Note that we accept payment through PayPal, then it’s easy for you to send us the Deposit, to hold your hotel/resort bookings. If you are paying by credit card online, your credit card will not be billed until after we confirm the availability of the hotel. You can pay us 30% Deposit in advance, then pay the rest 07 days before check-in date.

Can I make a reservation for someone else ?

Yes, you can make a reservation for someone else. Simply fill in the name of the guest for the booking and your contact details. You can also pay by cash, paypal or bank transfer for someone else. However, you cannot make a payment for someone else using credit card. The name on the credit card must match the name of the guest.

How do I know my reservation is confirmed ?

After you make an online booking, we will follow up with you to confirm availability and confirm the booking. Your reservation is not confirmed until you receive this follow-up email or fax.

I am not ready to book right now, what should I do ?

For flights, you can make a booking and then we can hold the booking for a limited period of time until you are ready to purchase and issue the ticket. When you make a booking, we will inform you of the amount of time until the ticket must be issued since this timeframe varies by airline.

For hotels, in order to make a booking and hold a room, you must confirm and pay immediately. Unfortunately, we cannot hold a hotel booking for you without payment.

For tours, as tours are related to hotels booking. We will need you 30% for Deposit. This amount is to cover our costs if you cancel the tour. Almost is for cancellation charges of hotels/resorts.

Can I make payment for someone else using a credit card ?

No, you cannot make a payment for someone else using credit card. The name on the credit card must match the name of the guest.
However, you can pay for someone else using bank transfer or through PayPal.

What currency your prices are in ?

All of the prices on our website are in US Dollar.

Do you accept credit card ?

Yes, We accept Mastercard, Visa Card and American Express.

How many payment methods do you offer and what are they ?

Now we offer 5 payment methods: Cash, Bank transfer, Credit card imprint, Secure PayPal online payment and payment through Western Union.

Do you have electronic air tickets ?

Very few of the airlines in Vietnam offer electronic ticket. If an electronic ticket is available, we will issue an electronic ticket instead of a paper ticket, unless a paper ticket is specifically requested by the customer. Electronic tickets will be emailed or faxed to the customer.

I just received my flight ticket and there is a problem with it. What should I do ?

Please contact the customer service team immediately. If your flight is less than 48 hours from the time that you receive the ticket, please contact a customer service representative directly by phone. If the flight is more than 48 hours from the time that you receive the ticket, please contact us by phone or email at booking@travelagencyvietnam.com.

What happens if I make an airline booking and then do not confirm or pay ?

When you make an airline booking, we can hold the booking for a limited period of time, which depends on the availability of the flight and the airline. If we have not received confirmation from you after two emails or faxes, we will automatically cancel the booking.

If you confirm the booking but arrange to issue and pay the ticket later, we will inform you of the deadline for payment. If you do not make payment before this deadline, the booking will be automatically cancelled.
